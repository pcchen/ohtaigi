Orthography¶

In this article, we describe the orthography of “Tâi-uân lô-má-jī”. Three variants will be included.

  • TL(o͘/ⁿ) variant.

  • TL(oo/nn) variant.

  • POJ (with Tiuⁿ,Jū-hông’s extension).
